## 📅 High-Intensity Action Plan (April - October)

### Phase 1: Dynamic Modeling & Daily Market Voice (April) - `IN PROGRESS`
* **Daily Macro Intake:** Select 1+ global finance podcast/article (MacroVoices, Grant Williams, etc.) and extract a **"Core Risk Thesis."**
* **Technical Engineering:** Convert the daily thesis into a SQL/Python data model (e.g., Testing liquidity, correlation, or volatility).
* **Communication:** Draft a daily 300-word **"Investment Memo"** in English, critiquing the daily intake with data evidence.
* **Deliverable:** GitHub Repository with an evolving "Crisis Library" (Code + Analysis Memos).

### Phase 2: Systematic Risk & Technical Interview Drills (May - June)
* **Hard Skills:** Implementation of **Professional Risk Frameworks** (VaR, Expected Shortfall, Stress Testing).
* **Technical Drills:** Daily Mental Math, Probability, and Statistical Inference problems (Standard NYC Quant Interview).
* **Behavioral Excellence:** Build a STAR-based response vault for 50+ interview scenarios, focusing on high-pressure decision making.
* **Deliverable:** Python-based **'Custom Stress-Test Engine'** capable of handling diverse macro scenarios.

### Phase 3: Networking & Global Market Positioning (July - August)
* **Strategic Outreach:** Initiate Virtual Coffee Chats with NYC-based professionals (Goal: 3+ warm leads per week).
* **Asset Optimization:** Tailor Resume/LinkedIn for NYC’s competitive landscape and ATS filters.
* **Thought Leadership:** Publish a "Macro Sensitivity Screener" on LinkedIn to demonstrate unique market perspectives.
* **Deliverable:** Optimized Professional Portfolio & A confirmed list of 50+ NYC target firms/contacts.

### Phase 4: Full-Scale Recruiting & Performance Pitching (September - October)
* **Aggressive Application:** Execute cold-emailing and high-frequency applications for NYC-based roles.
* **The Pitch:** Conduct 3+ English mock interviews per week. Master the "Stock/Risk Pitch" under time constraints.
* **Market Readiness:** Refine a real-time "Market Outlook" covering current Fed policy, Yield Curves, and Global Liquidity.
* **Deliverable:** Secured interview pipeline and finalized compensation negotiation strategy.
